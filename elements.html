About Me

I love storytelling, I love data & I love storytelling with data.

Hi, I'm majoring in Computer Information Systems with a concentration in Data Analytics from the J. Mack Robinson College of Business, Georgia State University.
I am deeply connected to leveraging research, data, and storytelling to create meaningful impact through my work.

Ever since I was little, I have loved telling stories. I've always had a natural curiosity for statistics and research. Discovering Python and Tableau during my Computer Information Systems class was a thrilling revelation, as I have always wanted to work in this intersection where I could weave narratives through data to make an impact. This interest further developed when I had the opportunity to work with qualitative data and use ArcGIS to visualize my narrative with my Student Research Assistant position.

I'm working towards continuously expanding my knowledge in the diverse fields of research & data, driven by a commitment to be better than I was every day.
Thank you for taking out the time to read about me.
